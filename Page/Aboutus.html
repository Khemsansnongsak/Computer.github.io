  Goals
1.Customer Satisfaction:
    Objective: Achieve a customer satisfaction rating of 95% or higher.
    Implement a robust feedback system to gather and act on customer insights.
    Provide comprehensive training for staff to ensure knowledgeable and friendly service.
2.Product Range Expansion:
    Objective: Expand product offerings by 20% within the next year.
    Regularly review market trends to identify and source new products.

    Build strong relationships with key suppliers to access the latest technology.
3.Revenue Growth:
    Objective: Increase annual revenue by 15%.

    Develop targeted marketing campaigns to attract new customers.

    Introduce loyalty programs and promotions to boost repeat business.
4.Operational Efficiency:
    Objective: Reduce operational costs by 10% through improved efficiency.

    Actions:

    Implement inventory management software to optimize stock levels and reduce waste.

    Streamline store operations with updated procedures and technology.
5.Employee Development:
    Objective: Enhance staff skills and knowledge with at least 20 hours of professional development per employee annually.

    Actions:

    Offer regular training workshops and access to industry certifications.

    Encourage staff participation in tech-related conferences and seminars.
6.Community Engagement:
    Objective: Increase community involvement and support through local events and partnerships.

    Actions:

    Sponsor or host technology workshops and educational events.

    Partner with local schools and organizations to provide tech support and resources.
